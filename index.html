Your Homes Solar System financing options.

You could choose any term. Each loan has a loan fee that has been calculated in the price. You will get a tax rebate on the loan fee as part of your total solar system cost. Your payments are calculated assuming that you will apply your tax rebate to your systems price. Once you do that your monthly payments will automatically recalculate.
